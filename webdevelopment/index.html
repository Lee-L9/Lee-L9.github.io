About this Website

This website is a space to showcase my journey in computer science, highlighting my projects and experiences in software engineering. My goal is to use my skills to solve real-world problems while, at the same time, promoting inclusivity in tech. Here, you will find a collection of projects that shows my abilities in coding, problem-solving, and creative thinking. Outside of computing, I think highly of collaboration and knowledge sharing; whether it be through clubs, mentorship, or teamwork. I hope this website will give you a deeper understanding of my progress and my passion and help me make connections with people that are like-minded. No matter if you are here to check out my journey, collaborate, or just get to know me, feel free to look around and reach out to me. We might be able to create something meaningful together.
